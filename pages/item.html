About This Pastry

Our Pain au Chocolat is a testament to traditional French pastry-making. Each piece
begins
with our signature croissant dough, made with premium European butter and carefully laminated to create dozens of delicate layers. We then encase two bars of finest Belgian dark chocolate before shaping and proofing overnight.

The baking process is crucial - our master bakers monitor each batch to ensure the perfect golden color and optimal flakiness. The result is a pastry that's crispy on the outside, tender and buttery on the inside, with rich chocolate that melts perfectly with each
bite.

Best enjoyed warm, our Pain au Chocolat pairs beautifully with coffee or can be savored on its own as the perfect morning treat or afternoon indulgence.
